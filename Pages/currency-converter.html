Currency Converter

Convert between different currencies using real-time exchange rates to plan your trades effectively.

How to Use the Currency Converter

  1. Enter the amount you want to convert in the "Amount" field
  2. Select the currency you're converting from in the "From Currency" dropdown
  3. Select the currency you want to convert to in the "To Currency" dropdown
  4. Use the swap button to quickly reverse the conversion
  5. Click "Convert Currency" to see the result
